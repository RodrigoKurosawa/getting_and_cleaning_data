'final_df.txt' description

Column 1: 'Subject'
Identification of the subject who performed the activity (Range from 1 to 30)

Column 2: 'Activity'
Type of the activity performed.
(6 types: laying, sitting, standing, walking, walking downstairs, walking upstairs)

Columns 3 to 68:
The raw dataset contains measurements of variables for each subject and each activity.
This dataset contains the average of each variable for each subject and each acitity.
The variables chosen for this dataset are:

tBodyAcc-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
tGravityAcc-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
tBodyAccJerk-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
tBodyGyro-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
tBodyGyroJerk-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
tBodyAccMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
tGravityAccMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
tBodyAccJerkMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
tBodyGyroMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
tBodyGyroJerkMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
fBodyAcc-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
fBodyAccJerk-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
fBodyGyro-XYZ - 6 columns: mean() and std() for each axis
fBodyAccMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
fBodyAccJerkMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
fBodyGyroMag - 2 columns: mean() and std()
